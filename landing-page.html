The Client

Dr. Luísa Terzi is an experienced therapist specialized in Gestalt therapy. Recognizing the need to expand her online presence and reach a wider audience seeking therapeutic support, she has decided to request the creation of a landing page for her practice. With a clear vision of providing an informative and welcoming platform, Dr. Luísa aims to provide detailed information about Gestalt therapy, its therapeutic process, and the benefits it can offer to potential clients.

The Goal

Dr. Luísa is looking to invest in a landing page to establish a solid online presence that conveys her expertise, passion and commitment to her clients' emotional and mental well-being. She recognizes the importance of adapting to digital trends and offering a convenient way for individuals to explore her therapeutic services. Through this digital platform, Dr. Luísa hopes to connect with a diverse audience and provide an enlightening introduction to Gestalt therapy, encouraging those seeking support to consider this holistic approach to self-knowledge and personal growth.

Visual Design and Brand Identity: All of the client visual identity was maintained in the creation of this landing page, so that everything is very cohesive, ensuring consistency and brand recognition.

The Landing Page

The sitemap will be the visual representation of the landing page's architecture, it will have navigation by sections and the name of the sections will be linked in the navbar, thus allowing users to navigate directly to specific areas of the site, thus helping to organize and structure the content in a logical and intuitive way. Here is a description of how the sitemap will be structured:

Navbar fixed: The fixed navbar will enhance and provide users with quick and easy access to different sections and functionalities. It will provide users with quick and easy access to different sections and functionalities. For the client in question who is using a landing page, the content will be very succinct and with simple navigation, to introduce the client's user to the type of service provided and the method the professional uses.

Services: Will show the user how consultations can be made, which groups the professional serves and the benefits that psychotherapy brings.

Media: Will be the professional's summary section, so that clients and future clients can follow the visibility of the topics covered by the client in different media.

Contact: This section will be so that they can get in touch with her for quotes and appointments, as well as presenting the professional's most active social network.
