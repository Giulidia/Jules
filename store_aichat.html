The Company

It is one of the largest and most recognized retail brands in Portugal, specializing in technology, consumer electronics, and home appliances. Present in the daily lives of millions of people, it combines a strong network of physical stores with a robust digital platform, delivering a simple, accessible, and fully integrated shopping experience.

The goal is to refine the AI chat that centralizes and integrates all store information to support sellers nationwide.

Analysis

The Product: The AI Chat was created to centralize critical store information in a single intelligent tool, empowering frontline teams with fast, reliable access to operational, commercial, and product insights. By reducing information fragmentation and dependency on multiple systems, it supports better decision-making and enables sellers to focus on delivering a consistent, high-quality customer experience across all stores.

The scenario: The AI Chat was launched without a strong rollout moment or structured adoption strategy, which resulted in low visibility and limited user engagement. As a consequence, only a small number of users actively adopted the feature, preventing it from reaching its full potential and the expected business impact.

In response, the business, together with the Product Owner and Product Manager, sought to understand the underlying reasons for this low adoption. It became essential to assess whether the issue was related to feature discoverability, unclear value proposition, ingrained habits from the previous tool, or friction within the user experience itself. This understanding was particularly critical given that the AI Chat was designed to replace a legacy tool and become a central support mechanism in users‚Äô daily workflows.

Therefore, the challenge went beyond measuring usage metrics. It required a deeper understanding of user behavior, expectations, and resistance to change, in order to inform refinement decisions that would drive more natural, consistent adoption and ensure alignment with both product and business goals.

Methodology: To investigate the low adoption of the AI Chat and identify opportunities for improvement, a data-driven research methodology was applied, combining quantitative behavioral analysis with qualitative insight inference through Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity.

Visual Design and Brand Identity: All of the company's visual identity was maintained in the creation of the ecosystem, so that everything is very cohesive, ensuring consistency and brand recognition across all products.

Mobile x Desktop

This product has both mobile and desktop versions, adaptable to the needs of different contexts of use and designed to offer a user experience regardless of the platform used.

The Product

Prototyping: In the navigable prototype, the left-hand side of the home screen has been separated into new product features and general updates. On the right-hand side are the products that the user already owns and are marked with a check icon. When the user clicks to access the Ecosystem, it shows the list of products that the company has to offer if they want to buy.
