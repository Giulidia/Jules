The Company

O-PitBlast is a technology company focused on mining and explosives, providing tools and functionalities that assist in the efficient management and execution of mining operations, especially with regard to the planning and execution of controlled blasting.

The Software

Surface: O-Pit Surface works in open-cast mines and quarries, allowing you to maintain total control over the results of explosive applications, accurately predict vibrations and efficiently manage fragmentation results, all on a unified and advanced platform. The primary function of mining and explosives software is to provide tools and functionalities that assist in the efficient management and execution of mining operations, especially with regard to the planning and execution of controlled blasts.

The Problem

The company had been using the software for more than 6 years, so the software's processing capacity was quite outdated and dated. The company had new competitors on the market who were a few steps ahead of their product. In addition, the mining-focused technology was constantly evolving and certain old features limited their ability to meet current customer needs.

The Solution

Navigation Improvements: Improve product navigation, making it easier for users to find what they are looking for. Reorganization of menus, the addition of advanced search functionalities and the creation of a more intuitive information structure.

Make it clean: Simplify the user interface to make it more intuitive and easier to use. Removing unnecessary elements and simplifying complex workflows can significantly improve the user experience.

CSD - Understanding

During meetings with sales staff who have been with the company for almost a decade, the ux team and the product team, the certainties were the guaranteed information and feelings that users have. Doubts were uncertainties to be resolved that the customer or future customer has towards the software. Assumptions are predictions based on previous experiences, such as: at trade fairs where the product is sold, what were the biggest questions future customers had before purchasing the software. This way we can mitigate risks and make more informed decisions during the planning and execution of the project in question.

Who uses?

Mining Engineers, Geologists, Mining Technicians and Operations Managers are among the professionals who rely on blasting software for their activities. The user journey of this software involves an integration of planning, execution, analysis and collaboration, ensuring the success of operations in open-pit mines. The use of blasting software plays a crucial role at every stage of the process.
