The Company

A technology company focused on stone scanning, whether marble, granite, ceramics, onyx, sandstone, limestone or slate. recognized the need to unify all its products into a cohesive ecosystem, with the aim of improving operational efficiency, simplifying processes and offering a unified user experience

The goal is to create a tenant that centralizes and integrates all of the company's products in an intuitive, user-friendly platform.

Analysis

Analysis of Existing Products: The company's 8 existing products were analyzed, identifying gaps, redundancies and integration opportunities.

User Journey Mapping: I mapped out user journeys to identify touchpoints and interactions.

Prototypes: I created prototypes to visualize the tenant's information architecture and navigation flow.

Visual Design and Brand Identity: All of the company's visual identity was maintained in the creation of the ecosystem, so that everything is very cohesive, ensuring consistency and brand recognition across all products.

Mobile x Desktop

This product has both mobile and desktop versions, adaptable to the needs of different contexts of use and designed to offer a user experience regardless of the platform used.

The Product

Prototyping: In the navigable prototype, the left-hand side of the home screen has been separated into new product features and general updates. On the right-hand side are the products that the user already owns and are marked with a check icon. When the user clicks to access the Ecosystem, it shows the list of products that the company has to offer if they want to buy.
